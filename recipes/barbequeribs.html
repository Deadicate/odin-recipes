Barbeque BabybackRibs


Description

The rootenest tootenest recipe for baby back ribs. Smothered in thick barbeque sauce, these ribs will have you crying for momma

Ingredients

  • baby back ribs - 3kg
  • barbeque sauce - 1 bottle
  • salt and pepper
  • vinegar

Recipe

  1. Let your ribs settle at room temp for 1 hour
  2. smother the ribs in the barbeque sauce, marinade for 1 hour
  3. Cook the ribs at 220oC for 2 hours wraped in foil
  4. Remove foil, and cook at 180oC for 15 minutes
