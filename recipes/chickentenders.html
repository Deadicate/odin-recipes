Chicken Tenders Recipe


Description

Succulent chicken tenders coated in buttermilk to give a rich deep flavour. Delicately cooked to preserve flavour and moistness

Ingredients

  • Chicken breat - 500g
  • Breadcrumbs - 150g
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • buttermilk - 200ml

Recipe

  1. Cut chicken in mouthsize pieces
  2. Coat chicken in egg, breadcrumbs, flour and chicken Seasoning
  3. Deep fry for 15 minutes, turning once
