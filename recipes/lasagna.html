Lasagna Recipe

Description

A delicious lasange recipe likea mamma used to make

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta sheets
  • pasta sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • cheese sauce
  • Basil

Recipe

  1. Prepare pasta and cheese sauce
  2. layer pasta sheets with sauce
  3. Cover with cheese
  4. Cook at 180oC for 1 hour
